Kindle eBooks Store

The Amazon.com Kindle Store offers a vast selection of Kindle eBooks so you can indulge in your love of reading.

Escape to another world in a science fiction and fantasy novel, try to solve a crime in your next mystery and thriller read, or search other popular genres like contemporary fiction, romance, biographies and memoirs, teen and children, religion and spirituality, and more.

Subscribe to Kindle Unlimited and read over a million eBook titles with or choose from millions of eBooks at the lowest pricesover a million titles are less than $2.99. Browse curated collections like Kindle eBook Best Sellers, New and Noteworthy Kindle Books, and Editors' Picks to discover your new favorite author or series, or sort by average customer ratings for customer-recommended and highly-rated reads.

Each day we unveil new Kindle Daily Deals for adults and young readers up to 80% off.

From mysteries and romance to sci-fi and more, explore new authors, books, and genres on any device. Shop Amazon's expansive selection, choose a title, and begin reading in seconds. Kindle eBooks can be delivered straight to your PC or Kindle, Kindle Fire, or your iOS (iPhone + iPad), Android (phone + tablet), and Windows devices through our free Kindle Reading App.

Keep your Kindle eBooks with you on your favorite device and lose yourself in a good book anytime, anywhere. Never be without a book.
